Manali: A beautiful hill station in Himachal known for snow-capped mountains, trekking, and nature.
Goa: A beach paradise in India popular for water sports, nightlife, and relaxation.
Jaipur: The Pink City known for historical forts, palaces, and rich cultural heritage.
Rishikesh: A spiritual town famous for yoga, Ganga river rafting, and scenic beauty.
Ladakh: Cold desert region perfect for adventure, biking, and high-altitude trekking.
